"Blé" means "wheat" in French, but is also one of the many slang words
for "money". It's incredible how many trivial French words are related
with money, and it's my educated guess that the same observation can
be made in many other languages. So, you see, plenty of words for
money, and plenty of people wishing they had plenty of money, possibly
without doing plenty of work... That's basically what this song is
about.

What I like in "blé" is that it suggests a connection between money
and work, since "blé" comes as a product of cultivation and
harvesting, although I admit to sometimes wish that this connection
wasn't necessary for me.
