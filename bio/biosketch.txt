Alexis Roche, PhD, currently leads the Automation team at Didimo Inc. A graduate engineer from Ecole Centrale Paris, he prepared his PhD at INRIA Sophia Anitpolis, France, and has now over 20 years post-doctoral experience in research and development in the fields of Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence. His career took him to several leading technology institutes in Europe, including University of Oxford (UK), CEA Saclay (France), ETH Zurich, EPF Lausanne and Siemens Healthineers (Switzerland), before joining Didimo in 2019.
